About

Marissa is currently a Graphic Design Major at the University of Massachusetts Dartmouth. She is from Bristol / Warren, Rhode Island and graduated from Mount Hope High School in 2021. She played on the tennis team all four years of high school and still enjoys playing, along with longboarding. She has always enjoyed art and design since she was young and wishes to apply these skills in her future career.
